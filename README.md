# plumber_app

A field service platform for plumbing companies, built for two primary users — the **salesperson / project manager** and the **field technician** — with supporting roles for **office staff** and **admins/owners**.

## Vision

Most plumbing shops today choose between lightweight tools that cap out as they grow (Jobber, Housecall Pro) and heavyweight platforms with $50–70K first-year costs and months-long onboarding (ServiceTitan). Nothing on the market serves **residential service and commercial project work in one system**, and nearly every incumbent gates sales follow-up automation behind expensive add-ons, neglects Android, and treats commission tracking and permits as afterthoughts.

plumber_app aims at that gap:

- **Sales-first**: speed-to-lead automation, good-better-best proposals, financing-forward pricing, and default-on follow-up sequences — in the base product, not an add-on.
- **Tech-first in the field**: offline-first mobile, two-minute job closeout, voice-native notes, and a photo evidence engine that works in a basement with no signal.
- **Hybrid job model**: quick service jobs *and* phased projects (milestones, change orders, budget vs. actuals, permits/inspections) on the same customer core.
- **Open integrations**: connect to any number of CRMs via a unified integration layer, plus inventory/parts, supplier catalogs, and accounting (QuickBooks Online first).
